Frequently Asked Questions

{% img button__expand.svg %}
How does Shotbud calculate session time?
When a user presses the “End Session” button, Shotbud will use the timestamp from the last shot detected as the session’s time.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
What is the advantage of using Shotbud over other shot timers?
For live fire practice, Shotbud uses Apple Watch's motion sensors in addition to the noise signature of the gunshot to verify that the user is the one discharging the firearm. Combining these readings helps Shotbud to eliminate false-positives.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Can I use Shotbud with only iPhone if I don’t have Apple Watch?
At this time, Shotbud requires an Apple Watch to be paired and connected to an iPhone.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Can I clear my data and start over?
Deleting and reinstalling the application will reset your data.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
How do I adjust the audio sensitivity or motion sensitivity?
Open Shotbud on either Apple Watch or iPhone, and navigate to the "Settings" menu.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Can I use my own photos for my Shelf?
We hear users requests for this feature and are including it in our development roadmap for future updates to the app.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Can I log scoring for a session?
Scoring is something we are actively looking into for a future release.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Is my data private?
Currently all data is completely local to your device and never sent to our servers. Eventually, we plan to store session data in the cloud to enable new features. Existing users will be able to opt-in to cloud storage at that time.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Can Shotbud detect dry fire?
Yes. In addition to detecting live fire, the user can enable Dry Fire mode (beta) in Settings.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Are there recommendations for how to wear Apple Watch while using the Shotbud app?
For best results wear the Apple Watch on your dominant hand.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
What equipment do I need to use Shotbud?
An Apple Watch, iPhone, and a firearm -- all safety rules and best practices apply. Airsoft or training pistols also work under many conditions although your mileage may vary.
