How does Shotbud calculate Session time?
When a user presses the “End Session” button, Shotbud will use the timestamp from the Last Shot Detected as the session’s time.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Why is shotbud better than other timers?
A key feature of Shotbud is that we use Apple Watch motion sensitivity in addition to the sound of the gunshot to verify that the person wearing the Apple Watch is the one doing the shooting, and not someone next to you.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Can I use Shotbud with only iPhone if I don’t have Apple Watch?
Not at this time, right now only able to use iPhone paired with Apple watch.

Delete and reinstall the application to reset your data.

Is my data private?
At this time all data is completely local to your device, and never sent to our servers. Although we do eventually plan to store session data in the cloud, this will be strictly 'opt-in' for existing users.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
What is the difference between Dry-fire and Live-fire modes?
Shooting live ammunition vs. Dry-Firing, which is the practice of "firing" a firearm without ammunition. That is, to pull the trigger and allow the hammer or striker to drop on an empty chamber.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
Are there recommendations for how to wear Apple Watch while using the Shotbud app?
For best results wear the Apple Watch on your dominant hand.
{% img button__expand.svg %}
What equipment do I need to use shotbud?
An Apple Watch, iPhone, and a firearm (all safety rules and best practices apply) airsoft or training pistols work under many conditions as well, although your mileage may vary.
